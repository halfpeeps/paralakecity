PERPHEADS 13th Anniversary Event

To celebrate 13 years of PERPHEADS, we have switched back to the map we started on, Evocity!

When the server first launched back in 2013 Paralake City hadn't yet been created. For the first year of operation, PERPHEADS ran on Evocity. For this event we have updated the modern gamemode to work on this old map. For many of you, this will be the first time you experience what classic PERPHEADS would have felt like, and for others it will be a trip down memory lane.

As well as the map change, the Anniversary sweaters have been re-enabled and are available for purchase from the clothing store. You can now also craft the Party Banners that were given out for the 10th Anniversary. (The original banners that still exist have been converted into a rare item with a unique design).

Things to note

PERPHEADS has changed a lot over the past 13 years, so there are some things to note while we are on this map:

  • Missions, including ChopShop have been disabled
  • There is no active mugging map, but rules around mugging still apply
  • Rules around base defences still apply but staff will be a bit more lenient, buildings on Evocity are not well balanced
  • This is a very different map to Paralake, you might need to rely on the map app to find your way around (like a new player :P)
  • A small version of the Casino has taken up residence in Izzie's Palace
  • You can purchase properties in the building named "Simply Estates" (Opposite the Nexus, or as would be known now, City Hall)
  • The map is not very walkable, and there is no public transport, watch out for pedestrians when driving
  • Bugs and issues are expected, though hopfully won't be game breaking. If you run into any major issues or need help, please make a report or support ticket
  • Go easy on the staff today, many of them are experiencing this map for the first time too :)

Have Fun!

We expect everyone to follow the rules during the event, but at the same time, don't take things too seriously. Look out for other players and don't abuse mechanics or law loopholes in ways that could spoil the experience for others.

We hope you enjoy spending time on Evocity, and thank you to everyone for 13 amazing years of this Awesome Garry's Mod Community!
